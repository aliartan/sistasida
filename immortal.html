Immortal Shieldbow

Stats: +55 attack damage +20% attack speed +20% critical strike chance +10% life steal

UNIQUE – LIFELINE: If you would take damage that would reduce you below 30% of your maximum health, you first gain a Hybrid resistances icon.png shield that absorbs 300 − 800 (based on level) damage for 3 seconds and 15 − 35 (based on level) bonus attack damage for 8 seconds (90 second cooldown).

MYTHIC PASSIVE: Empowers each of your other Legendary items with 5 bonus attack damage, and 50 bonus health.

This is Yone's main mythic that you go as the normal build because it helps him survive sticky situations. you will go thsi item in a majority of your games.
